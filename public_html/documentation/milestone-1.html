Personal Portfolio Website

Purpose:

  • A professional portfolio/resume website create to showcase competencies and front-end web development skill.

Audience:

  • Targeted professionals audience whose looking to hirer and recruit a web developer or whose just want to know more about me.

Goal:

  • For potential employees to learn and potentially contact me in regards to employment. Also, to increase and polished my professional online presence.

Persona

Eloy Munch, 49

Technology

Devices:

  • Samsung Galaxy Z flip 3 128 GB
  • MSI Prestige 15 512 GB 16 GB RAM

Operating System:

  • Android 11
  • Window 10

Browser:

  • Chrome

Profession:

Eloy is the head recruiter for a mid-size company called, ListoAI. ListoAI is aiming to improve to the quality and efficiency of the household’s artificial intelligence. They are looking to hire new and experience full time Front-End Web Developer to join their team.

Attitudes and Behaviors:

Eloy is a very friendly and outgoing person. However, Eloy is a very busy person. He spent a lots of time looking at potential new recruit for his company. Eloy like to look for potential new recruit through online and social media. He will only spend some time to look a person resume and portfolio site before he decided if he will contact them for interview.

Frustrations and Needs:

Eloy does not need a person with high experience but he is looking for people with creativity and style. Eloy is also looking for someone who is a good team player. Since Eloy’s company is a mid-size company, he have a limited budget to work with in hiring new recruits. Eloy does not have much time and he is looking through many candidates in the online space.

Goals:

  • Is the person a good candidate for his company?
  • Can the candidate’s portfolio website and resume interest Eloy in a short amount of time?
  • Does the potential candidate fit Eloy’s qualifications?

User Story:

As a user, Eloy need to quickly determine if the person’s website interesting and he/she will be a good candidate for the position in his company.
