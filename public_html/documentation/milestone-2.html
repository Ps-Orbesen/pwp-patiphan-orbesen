Content Strategy

One page website, consist of the following contents. Starting from the top of the page:


Navigation Bar:

Top navigation bar that included “quick-scroll” feature. Whenever one of option in the navigation bar is clicked, the page will automatically scroll to that specific section on the page. The navigation bar will include the following clickable options: Who Am I, Things I do, and Get in touch.


Who Am I:

This section will be about Patiphan. It will be a brief introduction about myself with my name and my story. This section will also include, an image of myself on the left side and Social Media buttons on the bottom of the banner. The Social Media buttons will be links to my social media platform: GitHub, LinkedIn, Instagram, etc.


Things I Do:

This section will take up a lots of the space on the website. It will showcase my core skills, experience, and what I can do as a web developer. The section will have a brief paragraph of my experience as a web developer. All the technologies/coding languages that I know relate to web development will be shows in logos format under the “my experience” paragraph.


Get in touch:

A simple contact form will be centered on the bottom of the page. The contact form will ask for user’s name, email, subject, and message. At the bottom of the page and below the contact form will include Social Media buttons similar to “Who Am I” section.
